## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this document is to specify the mathematical formulas and game mechanics that compose the **Scoring Engine** of LifeOS. Moving all scoring logic into this single source of truth ensures consistency across analytics, planner adaptations, and gamification modules.

---

## 2. Calculation Specifications

### 2.1 Recovery Score ($RS$)
Formula defined in [2.4_User_States.md](file:///d:/LifeOS/Product/02_Master_PRD/2.4_User_States.md#L30-L55).

### 2.2 Daily Consistency Score ($CS$)
- **Formula:**
  $$CS_{day} = (W_{tasks} \times TaskCompletionRate) + (W_{habits} \times HabitCompletionRate)$$
- **Weights:** $W_{tasks} = 60\%$, $W_{habits} = 40\%$.
- **Exceptions:** On 12-Hour shifts or Burnout Risk states, $W_{tasks}$ scales to $0\%$, and consistency score is determined 100% by core health habits (sleep check-in, hydration, smoking limits).

### 2.3 Focus Score ($FS$)
- **Formula:**
  $$FS_{week} = \left( \frac{\text{Actual Deep Work Hours logged}}{\text{Weekly Focus Target Hours}} \right) \times 100$$
- Target: Default = 10 Hours/Week.

### 2.4 Overall Health Score ($HS$)
A composite index (0–100) tracking weekly physical and mental stability:
$$HS = (S_{sleep} \times 0.3) + (S_{habits} \times 0.3) + (S_{stress} \times 0.2) + (S_{screentime} \times 0.2)$$
Where:
- $S_{sleep}$ = Average weekly sleep score.
- $S_{habits}$ = Positive habits completed vs. target.
- $S_{stress}$ = $(11 - \text{Average weekly stress rating}) \times 10$.
- $S_{screentime}$ = If Screen Time $\le 2$ hours/day, score = 100; if $> 2$ hours, subtract 10 points for every additional 30 minutes.

## 3. Gamification Mechanics (XP, Levels & Streaks)

### 3.1 Experience Point (XP) System
Points are rewarded for positive behaviors that reduce cognitive load or build long-term consistency:

| User Action | XP Reward | Naming Convention |
|---|---|---|
| Complete Daily Recovery Check-in | $+50$ XP | XP-REC-CHECK |
| Complete Daily Planner Task | $+10$ XP | XP-TASK-COMP |
| Complete Deep Work Session (25 min block) | $+20$ XP | XP-FOCUS-25 |
| Maintain Daily Win (Consistency Score $\ge 80\%$) | $+100$ XP | XP-DAILY-WIN |
| Log Unhealthy Habit (within limits) | $+5$ XP | XP-HABIT-LIMIT |
| Complete Weekly Review & Planning | $+200$ XP | XP-WEEK-REVIEW |

### 3.2 Level Progression
XP accumulates to advance user levels. The XP required for the next level is calculated exponentially:
$$XP_{required}(L) = L \times 1,000$$
- Level 1: 0 to 999 XP
- Level 2: 1,000 to 2,999 XP
- Level 3: 3,000 to 5,999 XP
- Formula for total XP at level $L$:
  $$XP_{total}(L) = \sum_{i=1}^{L-1} (i \times 1,000) = 500 \times L \times (L-1)$$

### 3.3 Streak Multipliers
- **Habit Streaks:** Logging positive habits (e.g. walk, stretch) for consecutive days.
- **Rules:**
  - Streak breaks if a day passes with a completion rate of $0\%$ for that habit.
  - Multiplier: After 7 days of consecutive streak, all XP earned from that habit is multiplied by $1.2\times$. After 30 days, $1.5\times$.
