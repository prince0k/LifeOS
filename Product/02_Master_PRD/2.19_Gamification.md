## 1. Purpose
The purpose of this document is to specify the **Gamification** design of LifeOS. It outlines the incentive structures, badges, levels, and positive reinforcement systems used to foster long-term consistency and minimize logging drop-offs.

## 3. Daily, Weekly, and Monthly Wins

### 3.1 Daily Win (REQ-GAME-002)
- **Condition:** Achieve a Daily Consistency Score of $\ge 80\%$.
- **Reward:** $+100$ XP on dashboard. Highlight today's calendar block in gold.

### 3.2 Weekly Win (REQ-GAME-003)
- **Condition:** Weekly average Consistency Score of $\ge 85\%$ AND Weekly Focus Hour Target completed.
- **Reward:** $+300$ XP. Display Weekly Summary report with a positive reinforcement banner.

### 3.3 Monthly Win (REQ-GAME-004)
- **Condition:** All weekly goals met for the month AND smoking count reduced by at least $10\%$ compared to previous month's baseline.
- **Reward:** Unlock special custom themes (e.g. Cyberpunk, Forest, Minimal dark).

## 4. Streaks & Multipliers

#### RULE-GAME-001: Habit Streaks
A streak is calculated as consecutive days of logging a specific habit at or above target levels.
- Streaks must be calculated per habit.
- If a user misses a positive habit day, the streak resets to $0$, unless the user was in **Burnout Risk** state, which pauses the streak instead of resetting it (Recovery Shield).
- Consecutive days milestones trigger multiplier rewards (e.g. 7-day streak $= 1.2\times$ XP multiplier for habit tasks).

## 5. Progress Indicators & Visual Layouts
- **Dashboard Header:** Displays current Level, XP progress bar, and active Daily Win status.
- **Milestones Page:** Local grid showing locked and unlocked badges.
- **Weekly Review Screen:** Renders streak progress animations and XP gained summaries.

## 7. Acceptance Criteria
- Earning XP triggers level-up transitions on the dashboard immediately.
- Pausing streaks during Burnout Risk works as specified by `RULE-GAME-001`.
